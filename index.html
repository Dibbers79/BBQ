What makes Elmhurst stand out?

The College seamlessly blends liberal arts learning and professional preparation to educate students for life in a global society and help them reach their potential. Our classes are small, and they’re taught by professors, not teaching assistants. More than 94 percent of Elmhurst graduates are employed or studying for advanced degrees within six months of graduation.

U.S. News & World Report places Elmhurst College fourth in the Midwest among colleges and universities that offer the best value for the money. The College also ranks in U.S. News’ Top 10 Best Regional Universities in the Midwest.
